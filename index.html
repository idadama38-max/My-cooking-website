Featured Recipe: Creamy Penne Pasta

A rich and creamy pasta dish made with penne and a smooth sauce.

Steps: Boil the penne pasta until soft. Melt butter and cook garlic. Add milk and cream, then stir. Mix in the pasta and add cheese before serving.

Tip: Add black pepper or herbs for extra flavor.
